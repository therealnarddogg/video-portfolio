Hi, I'm
Drew Bernard.

I'm a cinematographer & editor based out of Dallas, Texas.

I have over 15 years experience in professional video production, specializing in directing, cinematography, editing, color correction, audio, and lighting.

Through my roles at Freese and Nichols, Ruffwear, the University of Oklahoma, and my own wedding videography business, I have worked on a wide range of video projects. Whether it's educational content, highly-produced narratives, fun social media content, or cinematic wedding films, I am comfortable with whatever kind of video project comes my way.

In my free time, I enjoy fly fishing, skiing, gaming, and traveling with my family.
